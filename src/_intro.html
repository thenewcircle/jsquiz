This quiz tests your JavaScript proficiency. A score of less than 80% means you might have some trouble with the AngularJS course. A score of less than 70% means you will have trouble with the course.

Each question will appear on its own screen. Answer the question, then press the Submit button to submit your answer. You'll be told whether you answered the question successfully or not. Then, a Next button will appear. Press that button to proceed to the next question.

Some questions require you to enter some JavaScript code. Those questions will have a Test Your Answer button you can use to determine whether your answer is correct before pressing the Submit button to submit the answer.
